How to set up the Orka side of your AWS-Orka tunnel to enable two-way access between your AWS and Orka VMs.

> #### **You need:**
> 
>   * Active VPN connection to your Orka cluster.
>   * Your Cisco ASAv connection information from the [IP Plan](https://support.macstadium.com/hc/en-us/articles/28230867289883).
>   * The downloaded and prepared [VPN configuration file](https://support.macstadium.com/hc/en-us/articles/28401474359835).
> 


After you [have created your VPN tunnel in Amazon](https://support.macstadium.com/hc/en-us/articles/28401777655707) and you [have prepared the configuration file](https://support.macstadium.com/hc/en-us/articles/28401474359835), you need to apply the configuration to the Cisco ASAv in your Orka cluster.

  1. [Run Cisco ASDM-IDM and log in.](https://support.macstadium.com/hc/en-us/articles/28231599395355)
  2. In the Cisco ASDM-IDM application toolbar, select **Tools** > **Command Line Interface...**.



![a56dcdd-cisco-asdm-tools-menu \(1\).png](https://support.macstadium.com/hc/article_attachments/28401292351259)

  3. Select **Multiple Line**.
  4. Type [configure terminal](https://www.cisco.com/c/m/en_us/techdoc/dc/reference/cli/n5k/commands/configure-terminal.html), press `Enter`, and paste the contents of the [prepared VPN configuration file](https://support.macstadium.com/hc/en-us/articles/28401474359835).
  5. Click **Send**.
