At MacStadium, data privacy is paramount. MacStadium engineers do not have access to an individual Mac mini environment once a subscription is activated. However, this level of data privacy means that there are a few simple steps to follow to allow MacStadium engineers to reclaim use of the machine.

1\. (Suggested) [Backup your data](https://support.apple.com/mac-backup).

2\. Sign out of iTunes in macOS Mojave or earlier.

  * If using macOS Mojave or earlier, open iTunes.
  * From the iTunes window, choose Account > Authorizations > Deauthorize This Computer.
  * Enter your Apple ID and password and click Deauthorize.



3\. Sign out of iCloud.

### **macOS Catalina or earlier**

1\. Open Apple menu > System Preferences.

2\. Click Apple ID

3\. Select Overview in the sidebar.

4\. Finally, click Sign Out.

### **macOS Mojave or newer**

1\. Choose Apple menu > System Preferences.

2\. Click iCloud

3\. Click Sign Out.

**NOTE** : Your iCloud data remains in iCloud and on any other devices that are signed in to iCloud with your Apple ID.
