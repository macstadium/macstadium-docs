MacStadium offers a selection of Bare Metal Mac hosts for various applications. Our [benchmarks page](/iaas/bare-metal-macs/macstadium-bare-metal-mac-benchmarks) offers a detailed breakdown of the performance differences between available machines.

## Cloud-Hosted Mac Models

### Mac mini

Mac mini hardware is recommended for most applications, and offers excellent performance for CI/CD and virtual desktop workloads. Standard hardware configurations are available to purchase instantly on the [customer portal](https://portal.macstadium.com/register).

**Need Intel Macs?** Intel Mac mini hardware is currently available for custom orders. [Contact MacStadium sales](https://www.macstadium.com/contact/sales) for more.

### Mac Studio

Mac Studio hardware is recommended for GPU-intensive or high-RAM applications, such as video rendering or AI/ML inference. Standard hardware configurations are available to purchase instantly on the [customer portal](https://portal.macstadium.com/register).
