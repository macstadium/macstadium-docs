If you are registering and have an immediate need for five or more machines, you will first need to verify your billing information before you can order them.

After your registration is complete, you can either complete a single subscription or go to the MacStadium Portal to add additional machines to your account. If you need five or more machines at once and can provide the technical specifications for each Mac (or you need something not shown in the configuration wizard), open a support ticket with the Other type and submit your request.

For customers who add new subscriptions once a week or more slowly, the regular configuration tool is the best approach to spinning up individual machines as it gives our system the ability to instantly provision minis already staged.

If you want a new machine with the exact same software image as an existing subscription, you can have the source machine cloned to any number of other machines by opening a support ticket.
